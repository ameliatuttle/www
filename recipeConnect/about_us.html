our Mission.

at recipeConnect we've made a user friendly interface where you can easily find EGGSellent recipes based on 5 criteria: cost, time, taste, ease, and uniquness. anyone that uses a recipe will be asked to rank it on a scale of 1-10 based on each of these categories, and a score will be calculated. when you create an acccount you can connect with other creators and become friends. on your account you can message, share private recipes, and build you account score to increase the views you recipes get. we ultimately aim to help broaden your meals with cost effective cooking. now get out there and start cooking!
